C-H Activation Methodology

A facile, copper-mediated, direct C–H(sp2)/C–H(sp3) bond coupling of benzoic acid derivatives with ethyl cyanoacetate by the deployment of an 8-aminoquinoline moiety as a bidentate directing group is disclosed. Such a unique transformation provides a new strategy for the construction of an isoquinolinone scaffold as one of the privileged cores.

Chem. Commun. 2014, 50, 10634-10636.
